## 📄 Description
The objective is to analyze the **performance** of paid media campaigns across multiple platforms to identify which channels, industries, and campaign types generate the **highest return on investment (ROAS/ROI)** and **conversion efficiency.**

---

### Key insights:
- January has the highest revenue
- Sandwiches are the item that generates the most revenue.
- Iced coffee is the best-selling item.
- Most transactions are done in cash.
- Sales are distributed almost equally throughout the day.

---

### Final recommendation:
TikTok Ads is the most cost-efficient and profitable platform, suggesting that reallocating more budget to this channel could improve overall marketing performance.
