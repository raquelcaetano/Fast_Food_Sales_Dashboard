## 📄 Project Overview
This project presents a **sales analysis dashboard for a fast-food restaurant**, built using **Power BI**. The goal is to explore sales performance, identify customer purchasing patterns, and highlight the products and periods that generate the most revenue.

The dashboard helps transform raw transactional data into **actionable insights** that can support operational and strategic decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions
The dashboard was designed to answer the following questions:
  - Which **month generates the highest revenue**?
  - What is the **most ordered item**?
  - What **product generates the most revenue**?
  - What **time of day has the most sales**?
  - What is the **most used transaction type**?

---

### 📊 Key insights:
- **January** has the highest revenue
- **Sandwiches** generates the most revenue
- **Iced coffee** is the best-selling item
- **Cash** is the most used transaction type
- Sales are **distributed almost equally throughout the day**

---

### 📈 Final recommendation:
Based on the analysis, the business should focus on **promoting high-revenue items** such as **sandwiches** while **maintaining the strong demand for iced coffee**, the best-selling product. Since cash is the most common payment method, **optimizing cash handling** processes may improve operational efficiency. Additionally, as sales are relatively balanced throughout the day, **maintaining consistent staffing and inventory** levels across all periods can help ensure smooth operations and customer satisfaction.
